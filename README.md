# 📖 Comprehensive Guide: Using Uboor WA Collector Pro

## 🚀 Introduction

The **Uboor WA Collector Pro** is a professional and free Google Chrome extension developed by **Uboor**. Designed as the ultimate tech companion for marketers, sales teams, and entrepreneurs, it extracts any list of numbers from "WhatsApp Web" with a single click and exports them into highly accurate, organized spreadsheets (Excel / CSV).

---

## 💡 The Golden Rule: How does the tool work?

The tool operates using **"Smart Visual Reading"** technology; meaning it extracts data from the list that is currently open on your screen at the exact moment you run it.
Therefore, you can use the tool for three completely different purposes based on the screen you open before clicking the "Start Extraction" button.

### 🎯 Use Case 1: Extracting "Group Members" (Most popular for marketers)

If you want to extract the numbers of members from a specific group to build a targeted database (Leads):

1. Open the desired WhatsApp group.
2. Click on the "Group Name" at the top bar to open the side menu (Group Info).
3. Scroll down until you reach the members list, and click on **"View All"** to pop up the full members list.
4. Now.. click on the **Uboor** extension icon, then choose **🚀 Start Extraction**.

### 📱 Use Case 2: Extracting "All Contacts" (Address Book)

If you want to take a backup of all saved numbers in your phone:

1. On the WhatsApp Web main screen, click on the **"New Chat" 💬** icon at the top.
2. A list containing all your contacts will open.
3. Open the **Uboor** extension, and click on **🚀 Start Extraction**.

### 💬 Use Case 3: Extracting "Active Chats" (Recently contacted numbers)

If you want to extract numbers of people you had previous conversations with (even if they are unsaved):

1. Stay on the **main screen** of WhatsApp Web (where your chats list appears on the left/right).
2. Open the **Uboor** extension, and click on **🚀 Start Extraction**.
   _(Note: In this case, the tool will extract the name along with the "last exchanged message" as the description)._

---

## ⏳ What happens after clicking Start?

1. The tool will slowly **Auto-Scroll** from the top of the list to the bottom, then scroll back up to ensure no number is missed.
2. Please **do not touch the mouse or close the screen** until the process is finished (it takes from seconds to minutes depending on the list length).
3. A pop-up screen will appear containing an elegant results table. Click on **Download CSV** to get your ready-to-use, filtered data.

## ✨ Exclusive Pro Features

- **Wa.me Links Generation:** Creates a direct link for each number, allowing you to start a chat instantly from the Excel file without saving the number to your phone.
- **Profile Pictures Extraction:** Fetches the profile picture URL for each account (if available).
- **Smart Filtering & Cleaning:** Automatically ignores annoying noise words (e.g., Group Admin, typing..., message times) to keep your client data 100% clean.
- **Business Detection:** Identifies numbers using "WhatsApp Business", helping you target B2B companies effectively.
- **Geographic Country Profiling:** Automatically detects the country of the number based on its international calling code (supports over 160 countries).
